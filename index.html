Frequently Asked Questions

What is debt settlement?

Debt settlement is a process where you negotiate with lenders to pay a reduced amount to settle your outstanding debt. It's an alternative to bankruptcy that may help you become debt-free faster.

Is debt settlement legal in India?

Yes, debt settlement is completely legal in India. RBI guidelines allow for negotiated settlements between borrowers and lenders. FairPay Solutions provides guidance on how to navigate this process legally.

How much can I potentially save?

Depending on your situation, structured debt settlement may help reduce your outstanding amount by 40-60%. Results vary based on individual circumstances, loan type, and lender policies.

Will settlement affect my credit score?

Debt settlement may temporarily impact your credit score. However, we provide guidance on rebuilding your CIBIL score after settlement to help you recover financially.
